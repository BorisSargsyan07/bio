Grilled Flank Steak

Do you know,how to cook grilled flank steak ?
Here is the recepie.
To make it You don’t need a long, tender lovin’ session to get flank steak going: the cut is nice and thin, so it cooks pretty quickly. This recipe only uses a handful of ingredients -- red onion, cilantro, lime and chile -- yet somehow manages to deliver a wallop of flavor.

Pork Belly Slices

Ingredients

500g pork belly slices

For the Sauce

  • 4 tbsp ketchup
  • 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tbsp light brown sugar
  • 1 tbsp cider vinegar
  • 1 tbsp dark soy sauce
  • 1 tsp English mustard
  • 1 clove garlic, crushed

Method

  1. Heat the oven to 160C/fan 140C/gas mark 4. Dry the belly slices with kitchen paper, then season with salt and pepper. Mix together the ingredients for the sauce and toss the slices in the sauce in a roasting tin. Cover tightly with foil and roast for 1hr 30 mins, until completely tender.
  2. Remove the foil and turn the grill to its highest setting. Grill the meat for 3-4 mins, turning it halfway and brushing over the sauce from the tin, until the sauce is thick and sticky and the slices are charred. Alternatively, put the braised slices on the barbecue and baste in the sauce from the roasting pan as they sizzle away. Serve in buns with coleslaw
  3. Enjoy!
